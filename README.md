# TagIt Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

TagIt ("we", "us") operates the TagIt mobile application. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you can control or delete it.

---

## What we collect

### Account information
When you create an account we collect:
- First name and last name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Password (stored as a one-way hash — we cannot read it)

### Item data
We store the names and lost/found status of items you register in the app, and the QR codes assigned to those items.

### Device token
When you enable push notifications we store your device's Expo push token so we can send you alerts when someone scans one of your QR codes.

### Messages
When someone scans your QR code and sends you a message through the public web page, we store that message and deliver it to you by push notification and/or email depending on your notification settings.

---

## What we do NOT collect

- Location data
- Contacts
- Photos or camera roll (the camera is used only in real time to scan QR codes — no images are saved)
- Browsing history or analytics

---

## How your data is used

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Name, email, phone | Create and identify your account |
| Password hash | Authenticate you when you log in |
| Item names and QR codes | Power the lost-and-found service |
| Push token | Send you alerts when your items are found |
| Messages | Deliver finder messages to you |

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

---

## What is shown publicly

When someone scans one of your QR codes they see:
- The item name
- Whether the item is marked lost or safe
- Your name — **only if you have enabled "Show name" in Settings → Privacy**
- If you have enabled "First name only", only your first name is shown

You are in full control of this through the Privacy settings in the app.

---

## Third-party services

| Service | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **SendGrid** | Sends notification emails to you when a finder contacts you |
| **Expo Push Notifications** | Delivers push alerts to your device |
| **Neon (PostgreSQL)** | Stores your account and item data securely in the EU |

These services process data only to provide the TagIt service and are not used for advertising.

---

## Data retention

Your data is kept for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from **Settings → Account → Delete Account**. This permanently deletes your account, all registered items, all QR codes, and all messages associated with your account.

---

## Security

Passwords are stored using PBKDF2-SHA256 hashing and are never stored in plain text. All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS.

---

## Children

TagIt is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
